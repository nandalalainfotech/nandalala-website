DevOps


Devops is a set of practices that automates the process between software development and it teams, in order that they can build, test and release software faster more reliably. Devops is the combination of practices and tools design to increase and organisation ability to deliver applications and services faster than traditional software developmnent process. This speed enables organization to better serve their customer and complete more effectively in the market.






The main goal of devops


Improving development frequency.

Lowerin the failure rate of new.

Archieving faster timer to market.

Improving the meantime to recovery.

Shortening lead time between fixes.



DevOps Practices



DevOps is a union of principles, operations, and practices that improve an organization's ability to deliver services and applications. The goal is to streamline the software development lifecycle and deliver high-quality software.

DevOps is the viewpoint, customs, and having a set of high-tech implementations. It involves coordination among people involved in development, testing, analysis, and maintenance to ensure organizational success.

It involves a combination of customs and best practices aimed at fulfilling client satisfaction. DevOps plays a major role in project management and implements continuous deployment, continuous integration, and continuous delivery of modern software.
